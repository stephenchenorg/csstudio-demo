About HG Biomedical Your Trusted Health Ingredient Partner

和聚國際（HG BIO）成立以來，專注於保健食品原料代理與技術服務，致力為客戶提供具有國際專利認證的高品質素材。我們深耕營養保健產業，與全球多家知名原料廠建立長期合作關係，為台灣市場引進前沿的保健原料。 Since its founding, HG Biomedical (HG BIO) has been dedicated to health supplement ingredient distribution and technical services, providing clients with internationally patented, high-quality materials. We have deep roots in the nutritional health industry, partnering with renowned global ingredient manufacturers to bring cutting-edge health ingredients to the Taiwan market.

從原料篩選、配方設計到成品代工，我們提供一站式的專業服務，協助品牌客戶快速實現產品上市目標。無論是人類營養還是寵物保健，和聚都是您值得信賴的夥伴。 From ingredient sourcing and formulation design to finished product manufacturing, we offer a one-stop professional service to help brand clients accelerate time-to-market. Whether it's human nutrition or pet health, HG BIO is your trusted partner.
