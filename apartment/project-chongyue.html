崇岳 — 與自然共生的居住哲學Chong Yue — A philosophy of living with nature

「崇岳」座落於新竹竹北，背倚十八尖山山脈，面向遼闊平原。建案保留基地內 40% 的原始林地，以低密度開發原則，打造出竹北地區獨一無二的景觀住宅社區。"Chong Yue" is located in Zhubei, Hsinchu, backed by the Shiba Jianshan mountain range and facing vast plains. The project preserves 40% of the site's original forest, following low-density development principles to create a unique landscape residential community.

每棟建築不超過六層，採用清水模外牆搭配原木格柵，建築語彙與周圍山林景觀融為一體。住戶可享有私家花園、社區農場與森林步道，在都市的便利中擁抱自然的從容。No building exceeds six stories, using fair-faced concrete walls paired with wooden lattice. The architectural language blends seamlessly with the surrounding mountain landscape. Residents enjoy private gardens, community farms, and forest trails — embracing nature's tranquility with urban convenience.
