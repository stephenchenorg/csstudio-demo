瀚宇 — 城市核心的頂級複合住宅Han Yu — Premium mixed-use in the city core

「瀚宇」矗立於台北市中山北路二段，匯聚金融、文化與生活機能的精華地段。建案結合頂級商辦與精品住宅，以垂直城市的概念，讓工作與生活完美融合。"Han Yu" stands on Zhongshan North Road Section 2, Taipei, at the convergence of finance, culture, and lifestyle. Combining premium office space with boutique residences, it embodies the vertical city concept where work and life merge seamlessly.

低樓層為 A 級商辦空間，高樓層為私密住宅區，中間樓層設有空中花園與會所設施，創造出獨特的都會生活生態系。建築外觀由國際知名事務所設計，以雙塔對稱的形式展現莊重與大器。Lower floors house Grade-A office spaces, upper floors feature private residences, with sky gardens and club facilities on middle floors, creating a unique urban living ecosystem. The facade, designed by an internationally renowned firm, presents grandeur through symmetrical twin towers.
