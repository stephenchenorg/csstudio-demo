馥寓 — 都心的靜謐奢華Fu Yu — Tranquil luxury in the city center

「馥寓」座落於台北市大安區，緊鄰信義計畫區，以國際規格打造的都心靜謐宅邸。建築外觀由日本知名建築師操刀設計，以極簡線條勾勒出優雅的都會天際線。"Fu Yu" is located in Da'an District, Taipei, adjacent to the Xinyi Planned Area. This tranquil urban residence is built to international standards, with exterior design by a renowned Japanese architect using minimalist lines to create an elegant urban skyline.

室內空間採用義大利進口大理石、德國頂級廚具與日本衛浴設備，每一處細節都展現對品質的堅持。戶戶面向城市綠帶，讓自然光與綠意成為日常的一部分。Interiors feature Italian imported marble, premium German kitchenware, and Japanese bathroom fixtures. Every detail demonstrates our commitment to quality. Every unit faces the city green belt, making natural light and greenery part of daily life.
