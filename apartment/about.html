About Demo Development

Since 1998

以建築，書寫城市的未來Writing the future of cities through architecture

Demo 建設成立於 1998 年，深耕台灣超過 25 年。我們秉持「以人為本、以美為準」的品牌哲學，從土地開發、建築設計到社區營造，每一個環節都追求極致。Founded in 1998, Demo Development has been deeply rooted in Taiwan for over 25 years. Guided by our philosophy of "people-centered, beauty-driven," we pursue excellence in every aspect from land development to architectural design and community building.

我們相信，好的建築不只是空間的堆疊，更是生活態度的具體實踐。從選址、規劃、設計到施工，每一處都傾注心血，讓住戶在日常中感受到被細心對待的溫度。We believe that great architecture is not just a stack of spaces, but a tangible practice of lifestyle. From site selection, planning, design to construction, every detail is carefully crafted so residents feel warmth in their daily lives.

服務範圍涵蓋台北、台中、新竹三大核心都會，累計完工超過 3,200 戶精緻住宅，客戶滿意度高達 98%。Our services span three core metropolitan areas — Taipei, Taichung, and Hsinchu — with over 3,200 refined residential units completed and a 98% client satisfaction rate.

Values

核心價值Core Values

Core Values

精工品質Craftsmanship

Craftsmanship

每一寸空間都經過反覆推敲。從建材的選擇到工法的執行，我們以職人精神對待每一個細節，確保交付的不只是房屋，而是一件作品。Every inch of space is carefully deliberated. From material selection to construction methods, we approach every detail with artisan spirit, ensuring we deliver not just a home, but a work of art.

永續共生Sustainability

Sustainability

建築不應以犧牲環境為代價。我們積極導入綠建築標章、太陽能發電系統與雨水回收設施，讓每一棟建築都與自然和諧共存。Architecture should not come at the expense of the environment. We actively implement green building standards, solar power systems, and rainwater recycling facilities for harmony with nature.

在地深耕Local Roots

Local Roots

深入了解每一塊土地的紋理與城市的脈動。我們不做複製貼上的建築，而是為每個基地量身打造最適合的居住方案。We deeply understand the texture of every land and the pulse of every city. We don't replicate buildings — we tailor the best living solutions for each site.

History

發展沿革Our History

Milestones

1998

Demo 建設開發股份有限公司正式成立，於台北市設立總部，以「精緻住宅開發」為核心定位。Demo Development officially established in Taipei, positioning "refined residential development" as its core mission.

2004

跨足台中市場，於七期重劃區推出首件建案「璞真」，獲得市場高度好評。Expanded to Taichung market, launching first project "Pu Zhen" in the 7th Redevelopment Zone to great acclaim.

2010

累計完工突破 1,000 戶。同年取得首座綠建築標章認證，正式啟動永續發展策略。Surpassed 1,000 completed units. Received first Green Building Label certification and launched sustainability strategy.

2016

進軍新竹市場，於竹北高鐵特區推出景觀住宅系列，開創低密度豪宅新典範。Entered Hsinchu market with landscape residence series near Zhubei HSR station, pioneering a new paradigm in low-density luxury homes.

2022

榮獲台灣建築金質獎，品牌價值獲得產官學界一致肯定。全台完工戶數突破 3,000 戶。Won the Taiwan Architecture Gold Award. Brand value recognized across industry, government, and academia. Over 3,000 units completed nationwide.

2026

推出全新旗艦建案「馥寓」、「弈璟」、「崇岳」，持續以國際美學標準引領台灣高端住宅市場。Launched flagship projects "Fu Yu," "Yi Jing," and "Chong Yue," continuing to lead Taiwan's premium housing market with international aesthetics.
