Sustainability

永續發展Sustainability

Building a Sustainable Future

ESG

以責任守護，為未來而建Building responsibly for the future

Demo 建設深信，建築業對環境負有不可迴避的責任。自 2010 年起，我們即以「永續共生」為核心策略之一，從綠建築認證、節能工法到社區永續營造，全方位推動環境友善作為。Demo Development firmly believes the construction industry bears an inescapable responsibility to the environment. Since 2010, we have adopted "sustainable coexistence" as a core strategy, promoting environmentally friendly practices through green building certification, energy-saving construction, and sustainable community building.

截至 2025 年，已有 12 座建案取得綠建築標章認證，累計減碳超過 8,000 公噸，並持續朝 2030 年碳中和目標邁進。As of 2025, 12 projects have achieved Green Building Label certification, with cumulative carbon reduction exceeding 8,000 tonnes, progressing toward our 2030 carbon neutrality goal.

Pillars

永續策略Sustainability Pillars

Our Approach

綠建築設計Green Architecture

Green Architecture

從基地規劃階段即導入日照分析、通風模擬與綠覆率計算，確保每棟建築都能達到生態、節能、減廢與健康四大指標。我們的目標是 2028 年前所有新建案皆取得銀級以上綠建築標章。From the site planning stage, we incorporate solar analysis, ventilation simulation, and green coverage calculations to ensure every building meets ecological, energy-saving, waste-reduction, and health standards. Our goal is for all new projects to achieve Silver-level or above Green Building certification by 2028.

再生能源導入Renewable Energy

Renewable Energy

自 2018 年起，所有新建案皆配置太陽能發電系統與儲能設備。公共區域照明全面採用 LED 節能燈具，平均每棟建築年節電量達 15%，有效降低社區營運碳排放。Since 2018, all new projects are equipped with solar power systems and energy storage. Public areas use LED lighting exclusively, achieving an average 15% annual electricity savings per building, effectively reducing community operational carbon emissions.

生態保育Ecological Conservation

Ecological Conservation

在開發過程中保留原生樹木、設計生態滯洪池與蝴蝶棲地花園。「崇岳」建案更保留基地內 40% 的原始林地，成為竹北地區唯一獲得生態社區認證的住宅開發案。We preserve native trees, design ecological detention ponds, and create butterfly habitat gardens during development. "Chong Yue" preserves 40% of the site's original forest, making it the only ecologically certified residential development in Zhubei.

循環經濟Circular Economy

Circular Economy

施工廢棄物回收率達 92%，並與在地回收業者合作建立循環建材供應鏈。室內裝修全面採用低甲醛、無毒環保建材，守護住戶健康的同時減少環境負擔。Construction waste recycling rate reaches 92%, and we collaborate with local recyclers to establish a circular building materials supply chain. Interior finishes use low-formaldehyde, non-toxic eco-friendly materials to protect resident health while reducing environmental impact.
