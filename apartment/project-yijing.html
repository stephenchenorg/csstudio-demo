弈璟 — 光影中的當代居所Yi Jing — Contemporary living in light and shadow

「弈璟」位於台中七期重劃區核心地段，以「光影棋局」為設計概念，透過建築量體的錯落配置，讓每一戶都能擁有最佳的採光與視野。外觀採用大面積低輻射玻璃帷幕，在陽光下呈現如寶石般的璀璨光澤。"Yi Jing" is located in the heart of Taichung's 7th Redevelopment Zone. Designed around the concept of "chess of light and shadow," the staggered building volumes ensure every unit enjoys optimal lighting and views. The facade features expansive low-e glass curtain walls that shimmer like gemstones in sunlight.

公共設施包含無邊際泳池、空中花園、健身中心與多功能宴會廳，為住戶打造五星級的居住體驗。2026 年榮獲台灣建築金質獎最佳規劃設計。Amenities include an infinity pool, sky garden, fitness center, and multi-purpose banquet hall, creating a five-star living experience. Winner of the 2026 Taiwan Architecture Gold Award for Best Planning Design.
